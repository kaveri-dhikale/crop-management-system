Fertilizer Information

Urea

Rich in nitrogen, helps in leaf growth.
Used for crops like wheat and rice.

DAP

Contains nitrogen and phosphorus.
Promotes strong root development.

NPK

Balanced nutrients (N, P, K).
Supports overall plant growth.

Potash

Improves plant strength.
Helps in fruit and flower growth.

SSP

Provides phosphorus and sulfur.
Enhances root and soil health.
